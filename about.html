Our Mission

Our Vision

To become Southeast Asia's leading agricultural marketplace, empowering millions of farmers with technology and creating a sustainable, transparent food supply chain that benefits everyone from farm to table.

Our Story

Kebun Rakyat was born from a simple observation: farmers were receiving unfair prices for their hard work, while consumers were paying premium prices for produce that wasn't always fresh. The problem? Too many middlemen in the supply chain.

In 2024, we set out to change this. We built a platform that connects farmers directly with buyers‚Äîwhether they're restaurants, retailers, or individual consumers. By cutting out the middlemen, we ensure farmers get fair compensation while buyers receive fresher produce at better prices.

Today, Kebun Rakyat serves over 5,000 farmers across Malaysia, processing more than RM 2.5 million in transactions monthly. But we're just getting started.
